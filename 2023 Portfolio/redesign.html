Redesign

Website redesign

I recently redesigned the website I created in 2018. It was my first live website built with HTML and CSS, and the style and UX weren't as good as they could have been. That's why I decided to make a redesign of my first website.

Old to modern

I've rewritten the website with modern web design techniques, including responsive design, improved usability, and a better overall aesthetic. The website now has a cleaner, more professional look and feel, and the layout is better organized to make it easier for users to find what they're looking for. Additionally, I've added new features, such as image galleries and contact forms, to make the website more interactive and engaging. I'm confident my redesign will make the website more successful and help it reach a wider audience.

Product page

I included the prices on the product page, so the user doesn't need to hover over items to find out how much they cost. This makes it easier for them to select items based on their price.

Product detail

I added a new style to the product page, removing the borders of the extra product buttons and only using shadow this time, since this is enough of a visual cue to indicate that they are buttons.
