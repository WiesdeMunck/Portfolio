Ayurvedisch-koken.nl

Wordpress

I created a Wordpress website for a client of my freelance business. The website is a webshop dedicated to selling Ayurvedic cooking workshops. Ayurvedic cooking is a style of cooking that focuses on healthy food from the kitchens of India.

Cooking workshops

I needed a website that could sell workshops with a limited capacity and close sales after a certain deadline. To accomplish this, I decided to utilize the Woocommerce plugin since it provides both of these features.

Products

I created a functioning payment system utilizing Mollie in Woocommerce so customers could reserve a spot in the cooking courses. Additionally, I crafted instructional videos for the customer so she could create product and information posts after I had completed the website.
