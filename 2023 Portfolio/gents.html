GENTS

Advertisement

GENTS asked me to make an ad for their new clothes line. I brainstormed & sketched to come up with a concept that was creative & unique. The ad had to be eye-catching & stand out in the competitive market. After some work, I created an illustration that met their needs.

Adding new elements

I was tasked with creating line illustrations of GENTS' clothing line that were minimal yet distinct. I had to imagine each item in a visual form and keep them different enough from each other so the customer could easily tell the difference between items.

Adding a dynamic touch

At first, I proposed making only static clothing illustrations. However, I suggested adding a more dynamic option that would make the ad more visually appealing, yet still fit the current styling and so the man in the suit was born.

Posters in stores

The posters and designs I made are distributed in 20 stores across the Netherlands. I have been designing advertisements for GENTS since 2015, starting as an intern before obtaining a part-time job, then transitioning to freelance work.
